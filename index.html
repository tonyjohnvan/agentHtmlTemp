Questions & Answers

How Long Can I Rent a Power Bank?
Renting duration will be effective exactly 5 minutes after a power bank has been rented. A borrowing period that is less than 60 minutes will be rounded up as an hour. The day the power bank is returned to the station will mark the conclusion of the rental period. For any extra days after the rental duration, a fee of $6 + tax will apply for every 24 hours of keeping our device. After 3 days lapses, your rental duration has expired, and your portable power bank is considered purchased.
What is the consequence of holding on to the power bank?
After the period of 3 days, failure to return the rented power bank to a RedShare Power station will cause the power bank to be regarded as purchased. If this happens, a sale fee plus tax of thirty-five dollars USD ($35) will be charged to your credit card. The power bank can be charged with a computer, or a standard Micro USB cable plugged into a wall outlet.
Where should I return my power bank when I’m done using it?
You can return your power bank to any Redshare station that has an empty slot. Redshare Power Bank locations are available on the app.
How Is the Fee for the Lost Power Bank Calculated?
If you lose a power bank, please report the issue to us immediately to avoid any additional fees. We charge $35.00 for every expired/lost power bank.
Do i have to pay a security deposit every time I use a power bank?
User of the iOS and Android App are not required to pay a security deposit. An very small amount of authorization fee will be placed on hold when you rent a power bank from any Redshare Power Station. If the power bank is returned within 3 days, the hold will be automatically removed.
How much does a power bank cost?
We charge a very small amount of fee per hour for using a power bank. The daily (24-hour period) maximum fee for a power bank is $6 + tax. After the first day or 24 hours, you will be charged $6 + tax for every additional 24-hour period the power bank is kept with you. Power banks can be held for a maximum of 3 days. After 3 days, you will be charged a complete purchase price of $35 + tax.
What are the credit cards accepted by RedShare?
Gift cards are not acceptable forms of payment. Redshare accepts the following credit cards: American Express, Visa, Discover, and Mastercard. We also take debit cards.
When will my credit card be charged?
After you finalize the rental of a power bank, your card will be charged temporary with a very small amount of authorization fee. The authorization fee will be returned to you when you return the power bank to a Redshare station. After you return the power bank, your credit card will be charged the hourly rate for the amount time you used the power bank.
How can I request a refund?
Any refund request should be forwarded to a Redshare customer representative. Please call (929) 313-4334.
Are power banks safe?
Our power banks use a secure current that only allow electricity flow rather than data transmission. Thus, our devices could not collect any information or data from your device. We guarantee 100% safety.
After scanned and paid, unable to charge your device when the cable is properly plugged in?
When the cable or the device is not functioning, please return it directly. You will not be charged within five minutes. After five minutes, please contact us at (929) 313-433.
Can I get help if there is a problem with the power bank while I am using it?
Please feel free to contact us at (929) 313-433 for any issue such as unable to borrow or use. We will be happy to help.
What phone models work with the power bank?
The power bank has a 2-in-1 cable that works with iPhone models released after the iPhone 5 and is compatible with most Android phones. It also has a USB Type-C cable, that supports models such as the Samsung GALAXY S8, Huawei, Google Pixel, etc.
May I borrow multiple power banks?
An account can only borrow one power bank at once.
How do I confirm that the power bank has been returned?
Please plug the cables back to the power bank and place the power bank back to the station with the “return” sign facing downward. A tone will ring if the return is successful. You may also check the status of your borrowed power bank on the History page on the Redshare App. The return is successful only when the status shows “returned”.
