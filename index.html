ABOUT REDShare

Redshare Inc. is a New York-based company that provides charge-on-the-go-service solution. Using our smart portable power banks, the consumers can charge their cell phones while carrying the phones with them. Our devices benefit both consumers and merchants.

We provide the public an affordable, reliable, and convenient way to charge smartphones. It’s the fastest and most convenient solution for people on-the-go but out of power. No matter where you go, we got your back.

How to Use our Service

Download

Download Redshare App in AppStore or PlayStore
You can also use Wechat or Alipay

Scan

Scan the QR Code on the RedShare Station using the App or Wechat/Alipay

Rent

A RedShare Specially Designed PowerBank will popup and be ready to pick up for charging your phone.

Return

Once you've done charging, Place the PowerBank into any RedShare PowerStation allover NYC to end your session
