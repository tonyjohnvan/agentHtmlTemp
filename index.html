ABOUT REDShare

Redshare Inc. is a New York-based company that provides charge-on-the-go-service solution. Using our smart portable power banks, the consumers can charge their cell phones while carrying the phones with them. Our devices benefit both consumers and merchants.

We provide the public an affordable, reliable, and convenient way to charge smartphones. It’s the fastest and most convenient solution for people on-the-go but out of power. No matter where you go, we got your back.

Rent & return
How long can I rent a power bank?
A rented power bank can be kept as long as you want. Renting duration will be effective after five minutes when a power bank is rented. Borrowing period that is less than 60-minute will be considered as an hour. The day the rented power bank is returned back to the power station will mark the conclusion of the rental period. For any extra days after the rental duration a rental fee of $6 + tax will apply for every 24 hours of keeping your power share. After 3 days, lapses after the expiry of rental duration, your portable power bank is considered to be purchased.
What is the consequence of holding on to the power bank?
After the period of 3 days, failure to return the rented power bank to a RedShare Power station will cause the power bank to be regarded as purchased. If this happens, a sale fee plus tax of thirty-five dollars USD ($35) will be charged to your credit card. The power bank can be charged with a computer, or a standard Micro USB cable plugged into a wall outlet.
Where should I return my power bank when I’m done using it?
You can return your power bank to any RedShare Power Station that has a slot. RedShare Power Station locations are available on the app.
How is the fee for lost power bank calculated?
If you lost a power bank, please report the issue to us immediately to avoid any additional fees. We charged 35$ for every lost power bank.
Do I have to pay security deposit every time using the power bank?
Users of IOS and Android APP are not required to pay security deposit. An authorization fee of $35.00 will be placed on hold when you rent a power bank from any Redshare Power Station. If the power bank is returned within 3 days, the hold will be automatically removed.
Payment/Charge questions
How much does a power bank cost?
The cost per hour for a power bank is $1. The daily maximum rental fee for a power bank is $6 + tax for the preceding 24 hours. After the original 24 hours, you will be charged $6 + tax for every additional 24-hour period the power bank is kept with you. You will be charged for a complete purchase price $35 plus tax for power share kept more than three days.
What are the credit cards accepted by RedShare?
Gift cards are not acceptable forms of payment. RedShare accepts the following credit cards: American Express, Visa, Discover, Mastercard, as well as debits cards.
When will my credit card be charged?
When you finalize the rental of a power share, your card will be charged a temporary $35 authorization fee and will be returned to you when you return the power bank. After you return the power bank, your credit card will be charged the additional amount based on your rental duration.
How can I request a refund?
Any refund request should be forwarded to the customer representative of RedShare through (929) 313-433.
Technical Support
Are power banks safe?
Our power banks use a secure current that only allow electricity flow rather than data transmission. Thus, our devices could not collect any information or data from your device. We guarantee 100% safety.
After scanned and paid, unable to charge your device when the cable is properly plugged in?
When the cable or the device is not functioning, please return it directly. You will not be charged within five minutes. After five minutes, please contact us at (929) 313-433.
Can I get help if there is a problem with the power bank while I am using it?
Please feel free to contact us at (929) 313-433 for any issue such as unable to borrow or use. We will be happy to help.
What are the types of phone that work with the power bank?
The power bank has a 2-in-1 cable that works with models of iPhone released after iPhone 5 and most Android phones. It also has a USB Type-C port that supports models such as Samsung GALAXY S8, Huawei, etc.
Can I borrow multiple power banks?
An account can only borrow one power bank at once.
How to confirm that the power bank has been returned?
Please plug the cables back to the power bank and insert the power bank back to the station with “return” sign facing downward. The return is successful after hearing the tone. You can also check the status at the History page on Redshare APP. The return is successful only when the status shows “returned”.
